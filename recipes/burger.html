Burger

A classic fast food choice. Follow the recipie for an eleveated experience.

This recipe will not reinvent the burger but will attempt to elevate a few of its elements. From the spearcut dills to the provolone cheese. Have your burger at medium speed, instead of fast.

Ingridients

  • hamburger buns
  • hamburger meat
  • sliced red onion
  • sliced tomato
  • chopped lettuce
  • sandwich sliced provolone cheese
  • dill spears
  • butter

Steps

  1. Form a portion of haburger meat into a patty that is roughly the size of a palm.
  2. Put the patties over a hot skillet and cook on medium heat.
  3. After about 3 minutes flip the burgers one last time and place provolone slices over them.
  4. Once the cheese is melted onto the patties, set them on a papwer towel to cool.
  5. Put a bit of butter over the skillet so that it covers its whole cooking area.
  6. Place buns over the skillet until the inside of the buns are toasted.
  7. Layer the halves of the buns with the mustard, mayo, and ketchup.
  8. Add the veggies and the pickles to the top portion of the bun.
  9. Add the patty to the bottom half of the bun
  10. Form your burger!
