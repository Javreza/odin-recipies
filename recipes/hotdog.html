Hot Dog

Something cheap, fast, and tasty.

Having to make lunch in a haste? With 5 simple ingedients, theres no way to get this wrong. Luunch on the go is a 3-minute boil away.

Ingredients

  • hot dog buns
  • hot dogs
  • mustard
  • ketchup
  • mayonaisse

Steps

  1. Boil the hotdogs in water for 3 minutes
  2. Put the hotdogs in a few folded paper towels so they dry and cool.
  3. Spread the mayo and ketchup inside the hotdog buns
  4. Place the hotdog in bewtween the slices of buns
  5. Drizzle with mustard.
